# Other Research Material

Use this folder for supporting material that doesn't fit the YouTube
transcript or LinkedIn post formats:

- Podcast episode notes (host, episode, link, key timestamps + takeaways)
- Screenshots of newsletters/Substack posts
- PDF reports or slide decks referenced by experts
- Cross-platform content (Twitter/X threads, blog posts)

Naming convention: `<author-slug>_<date>_<short-description>.md`
